Waste Sorting Guide

Understanding the three-bin system is essential for effective waste management. Each bin serves a specific purpose in reducing landfill waste and maximizing recycling efforts.

Green Bin - Recycling

For recyclable materials that will be processed and turned into new products.

  • Paper and cardboard
  • Plastic containers (clean)
  • Metal items
  • Glass containers
xLearn More About Green Bins

Blue Bin - Bottles & Cans

For beverage containers that can be redeemed for deposits in many areas.

  • Plastic bottles
  • Aluminum cans
  • Glass bottles
  • Drink cartons
Learn More About Blue Bins

Black Bin - Landfill Waste

For mixed waste that cannot be recycled or composted.

  • Food wrappers
  • Styrofoam
  • Diapers
  • Broken household items
Learn More About Black Bins

How It Works

Our waste management process is designed to be simple, efficient, and environmentally friendly.

Step 1: Sort Your Waste

Separate your waste into green, blue, and black bins for recycling, bottles & cans, and landfill waste.

Step 2: Collection

Our team collects the sorted waste from your location on a regular schedule.

Step 3: Processing

Recyclable materials are processed and turned into new products, while landfill waste is safely disposed of.
